Help & Tips

Getting Started

Welcome to YTP Maker Reopend! Here's a quick guide to get you started:

  • Choose Video File: Click the "Choose Video File" button to upload a video from your computer. Supported formats: MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI, M4V, MPEG, MKV. Max file size: 100MB.
  • Adjust Intensity: Use the intensity slider to control the amount of effects applied to your YTP. Higher intensity = more MLG!
  • Generate YTP: Click the "Generate YTP" button. Processing takes 1-2 minutes.
  • Download/Share: Once generated, you can download your YTP or share it with the community!

Experimental Audio (MLG Sounds)

Enable "Experimental Audio" in the settings for added MLG sound effects. This feature is still in development and may be improved.

Troubleshooting

Video Upload Issues:

  • Ensure your video file is in a supported format and under 100MB.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Try refreshing the page.

Generation Errors:

  • Reduce intensity and try again.
  • Some videos may not process correctly due to format or content.

Tips for Best YTPs

  • Use short clips for best results.
  • Experiment with different intensity levels.
  • Share your creations with the community and have fun!
