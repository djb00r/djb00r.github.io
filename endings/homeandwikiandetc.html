Cubes

Green Cube

Player / Main target

Your main controllable cube. Most endings involve saving or dooming Green.

Red Cube

Enemy / Bully

Tries to push Green into danger. Many endings are about defeating, trapping or merging with Red.

Blue Cube

Revenge Seeker

Red’s old victim. Helps you push Red into lava, water or space for revenge-style endings.

Brown Cube

Chill cube in the cave

Lives in the cave. Has secret endings with sunglasses, boats, clouds, Baldi ruler and space.

Yellow Cube

TV watcher

Lives near the freezer watching TV. Has endings about TV, roof explosions, space and friendship.

Cat Cube

Yellow’s pet

Yellow’s pet cat lives in his house. It can drown, go to space, eat fish, become Baldi, get mad, and even push other cubes into the lake.

Pink Cloud Cube

Flashbang friend

Lives above Brown’s house. Touching Green creates bright flashbang-style endings.

Cloud Forms

Cloud Machine

Any cube can be transformed into a cloud version with calm or sad cloud endings.

Locations

  • Grass Cliff: Main fighting area where Green and Red start. Many push / boat / space endings happen here.
  • Brown’s Cave: Inside the dirt island. Brown, the bed, bedside table, ruler and pink cloud live here.
  • Secret Robot Wall: Dark patch at the bottom of Brown’s house. Touching it starts the robot invasion.
  • Cloud Machine: Metal box on the far right. Touch cubes here to enter cloud mode.
  • Freezer + Ledge: Wooden ledge near the water with a freezer. Freezes cubes for ice endings.
  • Yellow’s House: Big yellow house on the right ledge with a breakable roof and a clickable TV.
  • The Lake: Blue area at the bottom of the world. Almost anything that falls in here gets an ending.
  • Space: Very top of the screen. Throw cubes high enough and they never come back.

Modes

  • Chapter 1 – Cliff: Classic grass cliff with water, Brown’s cave, cloud machine, freezer, boat and Yellow’s house.
  • Chapter 2 – Lava Lab: Dark lab with a lowering platform, Green over lava, static Red watcher and Blue revenge jumps.
  • Chapter 3 – Cage Lab: Green is trapped in a rusty cage with a bomb timer while Red watches outside.
  • Cloud Mode: Triggered by the cloud machine. Gravity is tiny and everything turns into fluffy cloud shapes.

Objects To Get Endings

  • Boat: Sails across the lake. Landing different cubes on it gives boat endings.
  • Ruler: On Brown’s bedside table. Any cube touching it becomes a Baldi version (including cloud Green).
  • Cloud Machine: Metal box on the far right. Transforms cubes into cloud versions with special cloud endings.
  • Freezer: On the wooden ledge near the water. Freezes cubes into ice and powers the Big Eye and Mad endings.
  • Needle: Next to the cloud machine. Used to pop normal or cloud cubes for “POP OH NO” endings.
  • Robot Wall: Small dark wall under Brown’s house. Touching it starts the robot invasion ending.
  • Yellow’s TV: Click it to turn it off and make Yellow angry, or slam cubes into Yellow while he is watching.
  • Yellow’s Roof: Hitting it with Yellow explodes the roof into many pieces for a roof boom ending.
  • Cage Bars (Chapter 3): Break or escape them before the timer ends to avoid the cage explosion ending.
  • Lava (Chapter 2): Slowly melts Green if he falls in, or drops Red for Blue’s revenge ending.

Main Objective

Experiment with all cubes, machines, and locations to discover as many endings as possible. Push, throw, freeze, melt, merge, explode and rescue cubes in different orders to unlock new outcomes.
