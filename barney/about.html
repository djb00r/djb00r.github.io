Our Dino-mite Story

Barneys Burgers was founded by everyone's favorite purple dinosaur, Barney! After years of singing and dancing, Barney discovered his true passion - making the juiciest, most delicious burgers in town.

With love, care, and a sprinkle of dinosaur magic in every patty, Barney created a place where families can enjoy great food in a fun atmosphere.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and big flavors makes every visit a memorable experience. After all, as Barney says: "Great food is made with love and a big imagination!"
